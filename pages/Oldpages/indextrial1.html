Adventure Starts Here

At 173 Heatherton Scouts, we care about helping young people grow, explore, and thrive. Based in Heatherton, Derby, we welcome children of all genders, backgrounds, and abilities from across the area, including Littleover, Findern, and Mickleover. From outdoor adventures and teamwork challenges to learning essential life skills, Scouting gives young people the confidence to lead, the independence to succeed, and friendships that last a lifetime — all while having serious fun along the way.

Build Skills for Life

Our programmes are designed to help young people develop resilience, leadership, and practical skills that set them up for whatever comes next. Whether it’s camping under the stars, trying something new, or working as a team, every week brings a new adventure.

Join a Supportive Community

We’re proud to be a friendly, inclusive group powered entirely by volunteers. Many of our leaders started with no prior scouting experience — just a drive to make a difference — and found a rewarding community along the way.

We’re always looking for enthusiastic adults to join our team. Volunteering with us is flexible, rewarding, and a great way to build confidence, develop new skills, and form lasting friendships. With more support, we hope to expand and offer even more opportunities for young people, including new Squirrel and Explorer sections.

No matter how much time you can give, it truly makes a difference.
