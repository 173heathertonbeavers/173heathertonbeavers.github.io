Cubs (Ages 8–10)

Cubs are young people aged 8 - 10 who:

  • Master new skills and try new things
  • Have fun and go on adventures
  • Make friends
  • Are curious about the world around them
  • Help others and make a difference

Every week Cubs gather to take part in lots of interesting and challenging activities - earning badges, and having lots of fun along the way.
