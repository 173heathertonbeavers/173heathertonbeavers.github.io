Scouts (Ages 10½–14)

Scouts are ready for bigger adventures! Our Scout section focuses on leadership, teamwork, outdoor challenges, and community involvement. Scouts have opportunities to camp, hike, and take on leadership roles while earning advanced badges.

Typical Activities

Adventure & Camping

Overnight camps, hiking trips, and outdoor exploration.

Leadership

Lead teams, plan activities, and mentor younger members.

Community

Contribute to local projects and charitable activities.
