Beavers - Costs and Uniform

The first half term is a trial period for your child - they do not need to pay subs or buy uniform.

After their first half term, subs are £20 per half term.

They will also need uniform after the first half term - this is a beaver T-shirt or polo shirt and Beaver jumper

When they take their promise and are "invested" into the group, we will provide them with a group necker.

We also recommend that they have a camp blanket, regardless of if they are coming on camps or not. This is a way to display badges from Beavers when they move up to Cubs, and also display blanket badges which we sometimes earn in meetings. It functions as a memory blanket, and can also have swimming badges, guide badges and any fun badges they want on it. This does not need to be a branded blanket, or an expensive one, any will do!
