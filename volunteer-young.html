Volunteer – Young Leaders

Young Leaders are Scouts aged 14–18 who help run Beavers, Cubs, and Scout sections. You’ll develop leadership skills, teamwork, and gain practical experience while making a real difference in your community.

What Young Leaders Do

Assist Sections

Support Beavers, Cubs, and Scouts with activities and games.

Develop Leadership

Learn to plan activities and manage a team of younger Scouts.

Gain Awards

Complete leadership challenges and earn badges for volunteering.
