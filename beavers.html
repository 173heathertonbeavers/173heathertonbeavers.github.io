Beavers (Ages 6–8)

Beavers is the first section for young people in Scouting. Our sessions focus on fun, friendship, games, and activities that develop teamwork and basic life skills. Young Beavers can earn badges, explore the outdoors, and enjoy exciting adventures in a safe environment.

Typical Activities

Games & Teamwork

Fun games that teach teamwork and cooperation.

Outdoor Adventures

Mini hikes, nature walks, and exploration.

Badges & Skills

Earn badges for completing challenges and learning new skills.
