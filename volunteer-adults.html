Volunteer – Adults

Adults make our Scout group possible! You can volunteer as a Section Leader, Assistant, or support role. Full training is provided, and you’ll help young people develop skills, confidence, and have fun through Scouting.

Ways Adults Can Volunteer

Section Leaders

Lead a section of Beavers, Cubs, or Scouts and plan activities.

Assistant Leaders

Support Section Leaders in running meetings and events.

Support Roles

Help with administration, fundraising, events, and trips.
