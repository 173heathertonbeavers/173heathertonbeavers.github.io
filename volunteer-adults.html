Adult Volunteers

Adult volunteers help run sections, support leaders, and make the Scout group possible. Full training is provided, and you’ll make a real difference in young people’s lives.

Ways to Volunteer

Section Leader

Lead a Beavers, Cubs, or Scouts section and plan activities.

Assistant Leader

Support Section Leaders with meetings and events.

Support Roles

Help with admin, fundraising, trips, and events.
