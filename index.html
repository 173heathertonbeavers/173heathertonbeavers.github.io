Welcome

We are a friendly and active Scout Group based in Heatherton, Derby. We provide exciting activities for young people aged 6–14. Join Beavers, Cubs, and Scouts for outdoor adventures, skills, and fun!

Beavers

Ages 6–8. Fun, games, and first adventures.

Cubs

Ages 8–10½. Learn new skills and earn badges.

Scouts

Ages 10½–14. Big adventures and leadership opportunities.
