What is scouts?

Scouting is a global youth movement which aims to shape young people's futures through fun, friendship and skills for life. There are currently over 60 million young people involved in scouting worldwide.
Learn more about scouting here

Who are we?

At 173 Heatherton Scouts we pride ourselves on creating an inclusive community, and caring about each child's individual development.
The group is run by a friendly group of volunteers, and we are always looking for more adults to help us continue to provide opportunities for our young members
