Welcome

Welcome to the 173rd Derby (Heatherton) Scout Group! We are a friendly Scout group in Heatherton, Derby providing adventurous activities, skills for life, and community for young people aged 6–14. Join us for fun, friendship, and exciting opportunities.

Our Young People Sections

Beavers

Ages 6–8 – Fun and play packed sessions with games, crafts, and teamwork.

Cubs

Ages 8–10½ – Challenge badges, outdoor activities and new skills to earn.

Scouts

Ages 10½–14 – Outdoor adventures, leadership, and community projects.

Volunteer With Us

Whether you’re a young person wanting to help as a Young Leader or an Adult interested in volunteering, you can make a real difference. Support our group and help young people grow with Scouts!
