Cubs (Ages 8–10½)

Cubs is the next step for young people progressing from Beavers. Cubs develop new skills, take on challenges, and explore adventure activities. Sessions include teamwork, community projects, and opportunities to earn a wide range of badges.

Typical Activities

Adventure & Camping

Overnight camps, hiking trips, and outdoor exploration.

Leadership

Lead teams, plan activities, and mentor younger members.

Community

Contribute to local projects and charitable activities.
